About

I'm a junior frontend developer with 1.5 years of professional experience. I started with a coding bootcamp in 2024 at Manda Hill Excellence Hub, after my time learning at the institution was cut short, I started teaching myself HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for a year. I built projects, contributed to open source, and then started a freelance campaign on LinkedIn where I have put my skills to the test, averaging 1 client per month since. My focus is React, TypeScript, and building interfaces that are easy to use.

I'm fascinated by performance optimization—especially why web apps get slow and how to fix them. I spend time reading other people's code, writing about what I learn, and pushing myself to solve harder problems.

Tools I use every day include Git, Chrome DevTools, and Figma. I also have experience with Webpack and npm for building and publishing projects. version control, debugging, and design collaboration are all crucial parts of my workflow.

Right now I'm diving into Next.js and exploring how to think about system design before jumping into code.

I am also trying to get myself to do more of Back-End development, I have a working knowledge of Node.js and Express, and I am currently learning MySQL and MongoDB. I want to be a well-rounded developer who can build full-stack applications and understand how the front-end and back-end work together.
